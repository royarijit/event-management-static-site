Bridal Makeup

At Himalaya, we understand that your wedding day is one of the most important days of your life. It's a day filled with joy, love, and unforgettable moments. Our skilled team of bridal makeup artists is here to enhance your natural beauty and create a stunning, radiant look that will make you feel like the most beautiful bride in the world.



Why Choose Us:

1. Professional Expertise: Our makeup artists are highly skilled and experienced professionals who specialize in bridal makeup. We stay up to date with the latest trends, techniques, and products in the industry to ensure that you receive the highest quality service and the most current bridal makeup styles.
2. Customized Approach: We believe that every bride is unique, and we take pride in creating personalized makeup looks that reflect your individual style and preferences. We listen attentively to your needs, offering expert advice and guidance while working together to achieve your desired bridal look.
3. High-Quality Products: We only use premium, professional-grade makeup products to ensure a flawless finish and long-lasting wear. Our products are carefully selected for their ability to withstand tears, sweat, and the demands of a full day of celebration, ensuring that you look stunning from the first photo to the last dance.
4. Calm and Relaxing Experience: We understand the importance of creating a calm and relaxing environment on your wedding day. Our team brings a sense of serenity and professionalism, allowing you to relax and enjoy the pampering experience. We will ensure that you feel comfortable and at ease throughout the entire process.

Luxury Doli Cars

At Himalaya, we believe that every aspect of your wedding day should be nothing short of extraordinary. Our luxury car service is designed to add an extra touch of elegance and sophistication to your special day, ensuring that you arrive and depart in style, making a lasting impression on your guests.



Impeccable Service and Attention to Detail:

1. Professional Chauffeurs: Our team of experienced and professional chauffeurs is dedicated to providing exceptional service. They are meticulously trained in etiquette, ensuring that you receive a seamless and stress-free transportation experience. Punctuality, discretion, and a warm, friendly demeanor are at the core of our chauffeur service.
2. Customized Packages: We understand that every wedding is unique, and we offer customized packages to cater to your specific needs. Whether you require transportation for the bride and groom, the bridal party, or even shuttle services for your guests, we will work closely with you to design a tailored transportation plan that aligns with your vision and requirements.
3. Red Carpet Treatment: As you step out of our luxury vehicles, you'll be treated like royalty with our red carpet service. Our team will ensure that every detail is meticulously arranged, from the placement of the carpet to the assistance in alighting from the vehicle, creating a truly memorable and glamorous entrance.
4. Impeccable Presentation: Our vehicles are maintained to the highest standards, both aesthetically and mechanically. They are impeccably cleaned, polished, and adorned with tasteful decorations to match your wedding theme and style, ensuring that they serve as a stunning backdrop for your wedding photographs.
